ABOUT SATECH

Satech S.A.S., based in Rionegro, Colombia, develops sustainable projects with strategies focused on transforming by-products into renewable resources using technologies such as pyrolysis and gasification. We also offer agro-industrial solutions with biotechnology, generating biochar, biofuels, and clean energy. Our contribution to the circular economy and emission reduction creates significant added value for our clients.

SUSTAINABILITY

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in the application of innovative technologies and environmental solutions to produce renewable resources and develop agro-industrial projects. With a highly qualified team in biological, biochemical, and scientific technologies, we aim to maximize the positive impact on the planet and generate financial performance for our investors and strategic partners.

Processes Satech uses to transform by-products and biomass into sustainable products

  • Pyrolysis
  • Gasification
  • Bioremediation; water and soil recovery
  • Composting
  • Waste to Hydrogen
  • Waste to Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF)

MEPI

Faced with the growing waste crisis, energy needs, and the urgency of decarbonization, the Micro Eco-Industrial Park (MEPI) proposes a tailored sustainable solution. Diverting material from uncontrolled landfills significantly reduces greenhouse gas emissions, supporting Nationally Determined Contributions (NDC) in the fight against climate change.

The production of biochar, renewable biofuels, and electricity within our MEPI project adds value: biochar improves soil fertility, sequesters carbon, and thus reduces dependence on fossil fuels.

Our dedication to the circular economy is reflected in the reuse of materials and the efficient use of resources, key factors for local economic development and the creation of opportunities in rural communities.

Advantages

  • Landfill minimization
  • Emission reduction
  • Carbon credits
  • Production of valuable fertilizer (biochar)

Economic Impacts

  • Reduction of energy costs
  • Sale of Carbon Credits
  • Profitable investment
